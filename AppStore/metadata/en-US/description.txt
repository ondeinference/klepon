Discover Indonesian food with warmth, clarity, and privacy.

Klepon helps you explore dishes, ingredients, and food traditions from across Indonesia in a way that feels calm, beautiful, and easy to understand.

Browse curated guides to iconic dishes, regional favorites, sweet treats, and essential ingredients. Learn what a dish is, what it tastes like, what usually goes into it, and what to try next.

When you want more context, Klepon lets you ask follow-up questions privately on your iPhone.

What you can do in Klepon:
- Explore curated Indonesian food guides
- Learn dishes, ingredients, and traditions
- Search quickly when you spot an unfamiliar name
- Save dishes you want to remember
- Ask follow-up questions privately on your iPhone
- Browse quick companion notes on Apple Watch

Klepon is made for curious eaters, travelers, diaspora families, and anyone who wants a more thoughtful way to discover Indonesian food.
